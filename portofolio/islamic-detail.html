Goals

Through this website, we are committed to promoting the uniqueness and beauty of the Samarinda Islamic Center to a global audience, attracting tourists to visit and experience a deep spiritual experience. This website is designed to be a reliable source of information covering history, architecture, facilities, events, and available services. We ensure that visitors can find all the information they need easily and quickly. By attracting more tourists, we hope to support local economic growth, create job opportunities, and promote Samarinda's unique local products and services.
